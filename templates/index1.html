FIND YOUR DREAM JOBS

The basic idea behind this project is to provide ease to people for finding the best jobs based on their skills that the person mentioned in their resume. It's very difficult for everyone to find out suitable jobs. The objective of this project is to suggest the top jobs from the dataset based on the skills of the person, first extracting the skills from the resume using pyresparser.
